GroundBnB is a housing rental service for people who need temporary shelter anywhere in the US.

Prospective renters can choose from our wide selection of properties.

Just select one of our available properties and choose your time you wish to stay.


Property owners can easily put up their own properties and receive a 30% cut for no effort on your part.

You may provide renters the key, or let us handle that for you.

Email us at groundbnb@ground.com for questions about our service.
